ABOUT TITIKSHA

TITIKSHA is a technical festival that has achieved great feats since its inception in 2008. The festival is held annually at Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University in Jammu and Kashmir, a highly technical and fully residential university that started functioning as an academic unit in 2004. TITIKSHA offers a variety of technical and non-technical events, entrepreneurial talks, and inspirational talks by eminent personalities. There will also be a game zone, adventure zone, musical concert, and an International Conference on Recent Trends and Advancements in Engineering and Technology. The festival expects a footfall of 5000 people only from J&K and attendees from around India are still counting.
In summary, TITIKSHA is a one-of-a-kind technical festival that offers a platform for young technocrats to showcase their skills and knowledge. TITIKSHA is a multidimensional congregation of young technocrats from not only inside the state but pan India. It is an event that witnesses a gathering of people from all over India and offers a variety of activities for everyone. It attracts a large crowd of young technocrats from all over India. Sponsorship opportunities are available, and the festival offers a great platform for advertising and brand promotion.
